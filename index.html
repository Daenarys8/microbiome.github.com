MicrobiomeExperiment

The MicrobiomeExperiment is an emerging ecosystem of R classes, methods, and educational material for microbiome research, formally introduced in European Bioconductor Meeting (EuroBioC2020). It contains the following key elements:
  • Class (Bioc): MicrobiomeExperiment
  • Methods (R packages): mia for analysis, miaViz for visualization, and other toosls
  • Documentation: MiaBook
  • Community: see MicrobiomeExperiment channel in Bioconductor Slack
